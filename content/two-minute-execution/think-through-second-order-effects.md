## Thought

The first effect of a decision is usually the easiest to see. The second effect often changes behavior, workload, incentives, or expectations elsewhere. Thinking ahead means asking not only whether a decision solves today’s problem, but what it teaches the organization to do next.

## Question

What behavior could your current solution unintentionally encourage six months from now?

## Action

For one pending decision, write down the immediate result, the likely response from others, and the new pattern that response could create.
