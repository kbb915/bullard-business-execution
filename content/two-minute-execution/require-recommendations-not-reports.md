## Thought

A report explains what happened. A recommendation shows that someone has considered options, tradeoffs, and consequences. Requiring a recommendation does not mean the leader must accept it. It means the conversation begins with ownership instead of escalation.

## Question

Do your managers bring you decisions to improve, or problems to absorb?

## Action

Set one expectation for the next week: when a problem is escalated, the owner must bring a recommended action, one alternative, and the most important tradeoff.
