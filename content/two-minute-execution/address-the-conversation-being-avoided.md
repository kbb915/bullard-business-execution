## Thought

Teams rarely avoid a difficult conversation without paying for it somewhere else. The cost appears as unclear decisions, duplicated work, guarded communication, or repeated frustration. Avoidance does not remove tension. It moves the tension into execution.

## Question

What important work is being slowed by a conversation your team keeps postponing?

## Action

Name the issue without accusation. Describe the observable pattern, explain its effect on the work, and ask the other person how they see it.
