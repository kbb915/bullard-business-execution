## Thought

Repeated reminders can keep work moving while hiding a weak execution system. When commitments live in one leader’s memory, follow-through depends on that leader continuing to chase. Visible ownership and agreed checkpoints transfer responsibility back to the person doing the work.

## Question

Which commitments move only because someone senior remembers to ask about them?

## Action

Choose one recurring commitment and make the owner, due point, and next checkpoint visible to everyone involved. At the checkpoint, ask the owner to report progress without being prompted.
